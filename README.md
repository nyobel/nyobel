## 📊 Highlight Projects  

### 📉 Customer Churn & Revenue Impact Analysis
SQL | SSMS | DAX | POWER BI

Conducted end-to-end analysis on a telecom customer dataset (7,043 records) to diagnose churn drivers and quantify financial impact.

**Key Outcomes:**
- Identified overall churn rate of **26.54%**
- Quantified revenue risk: **~$139K monthly revenue loss**
- Segmented churn by contract type, tenure, pricing tiers, payment method, and service adoption
- Discovered high-risk segments:
  - Month-to-month contracts (43% churn)
  - New customers (0–12 months → 47% churn)
  - Electronic check users (45% churn)
  - Fiber optic users (42% churn)
- Estimated customer lifetime value using tenure and charge patterns

**Business Focus:** Retention strategy insights, revenue protection, customer lifecycle optimization.
